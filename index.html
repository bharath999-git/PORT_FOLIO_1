WHO AM I

I'm someone who likes to bridge the gap between hardware and the web.


Most days, you'll find me toggling between simulating circuits in MATLAB and LTspice or writing Embedded C for microcontrollers, then switching gears to build out full-stack apps and UI/UX projects.


I really enjoy the mix of technical rigor—like power systems and Python scripting—with the more creative "vibe coding" side of CSS, making sure the tech works perfectly under the hood while looking just as good on the surface.
